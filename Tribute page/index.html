Steve Jobs

Co-founder, chairman and CEO of apple

Steve Jobs

Here is a timeline of Jobs life:

  • 1955 - Born in San Francisco
  • 1971 - Enrolls at Reed collage in Portland, Oregon
  • 1972 - Moves back to the San Francisco bay area
  • 1974 - Travels to India in search of spiritual enlightenment staying there for seven months
  • 1975 - Atended meetings of the Homebrew Computer Club
  • 1977 - Introduced the apple II computer at the West Coast
  • 1982 - Joined the Macintosh project after being forced out of the Lisa project
  • 1984 - Introduced the macintosh computer
  • 1985 - Submits a letter of resignation to the board of apple and founds NeXT
  • 1986 - Founds the spinout of the The Grapics Group (later renamed Pixar)
  • 1997 - Jobs is brought back to apple when apple bought NeXT for $427 million
  • 1999 - Becomes a board member of Gap Inc until 2002
  • 2003 - Was diagnosed with cancer
  • 2009 - Underwent a liver transplant at Methodist University Hospital Transplant Institute in Tennessee
  • 2011 - Died in his home due to complications from a relapse of his previously treated islet-cell neuroendocrine pancreatic cancer at the age of 56
